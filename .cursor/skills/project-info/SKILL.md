# Overview

This is a RTS Auto battler built in Godot 4. The overall structure is inspired by the Hearthstone battlegrounds mode, where there is a shop phase where the player buys cards and then when the shop phase is over it goes to the battle phase where the player is matched against another player in the lobby where armies are created from the cards they have placed on the board. A battle is simulated in realtime. When the battle is over the player who lost looses from the overall health pool and then it goes back to the shop phase.
